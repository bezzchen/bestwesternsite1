Directions
If you are coming from State Highway 1 Take Market Rd exit off State Highway 1. Follow Epsom sign to traffic lights (100 metres). Turn right into Great South Rd. Our motel is just 250 metres on the right (112 Great South Road, Epsom). If you are coming from the Airport along Manukau Rd Drive to the very end of Manukau Road, and then turn right to Great South Rd. Our motel will be on your left. (112 Great South Road, Epsom). If you are driving from Auckland Airport, Make sure you search us on GPS with 'Epsom' as the area, and drive towards 'Auckland' direction

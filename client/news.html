The Ultimate Auckland Foodie Weekend with Best Western Newmarket Inn & Suites, Your Perfect Accommodation Choice

For food lovers, Newmarket is a paradise of culinary delights. From world-class restaurants to casual dining spots, this vibrant Auckland hub offers something for every craving. Start your day with a delicious breakfast at Best Western Newmarket Inn & Suites, then explore these must-visit lunch and dinner destinations within walking distance.
Saturday Lunch: Asian Fusion Delights
For a flavour-packed lunch, head to White + Wong’s, where Asian street food meets modern dining. Their dumplings, Peking duck, and bao buns are crowd favourites, best enjoyed with a refreshing cocktail. If you're in the mood for Japanese, Ippudo Ramen serves up rich, umami-filled noodle soups that are perfect for a satisfying midday meal.
Saturday Dinner: European Classics or a German Feast
As the evening rolls in, enjoy a lively dining experience at The Bavarian. Indulge in crispy pork knuckle, giant pretzels, and steins of German beer in a fun, buzzing atmosphere. If you’re after something more refined, Bistro Eleventhirtyfour offers a European-inspired menu with top-tier steak, seafood, and an excellent wine selection.
Sunday Lunch: Laid-Back Dining with a Kiwi Twist
For a relaxed Sunday lunch, Ralph’s Bar & Eatery serves up gourmet burgers, loaded fries, and craft beers in a cosy setting. If you're craving something healthier, Scarecrow is a hidden gem offering farm-to-table salads, artisanal platters, and organic wines.
Sunday Dinner: A Flavourful Farewell
End your foodie weekend with a memorable meal at Newmarket’s iconic Archies Pizzeria, known for its wood-fired pizzas and authentic Italian pasta. If you’d rather explore plant-based dining, The Butcher’s Son offers a creative vegan menu featuring delicious burgers, tapas, and decadent desserts.

Located in the heart of Newmarket, Best Western Newmarket Inn & Suites offers stylish, comfortable accommodation with easy access to Auckland’s top foodie spots. Whether you're here for a weekend getaway or a longer stay, it’s the perfect place to rest after a day of indulgence.

IN THE HEART OF NEWMARKET
BEST WESTERN Newmarket Inn & Suites is located in the heart of Auckland's top Education, Medical and Business Centre. Just 5km from the CBD, and 800m from the popular Newmarket Shopping district making it the perfect accommodation option for your trip to Auckland.

BEST WESTERN Newmarket Inn & Suites is a 4 star Qualmark hotel, and offers spacious rooms with free WiFi and large flat screen TV's with free cable channels. All guests enjoy free on-site parking, and with our outdoor pool and entertainment area it is the obvious choice for quality accommodation at an affordable price for your next stay.

Accommodation options include spacious studios, suites and apartments. All rooms have built in head pumps and either a full kitchen or kitchenette. Ideal for individuals, couples or group accommodation.

Looking for a venue for your next Conference or Function? Look no further than BEST WESTERN Newmarket Inn & Suites. With our onsite conference room, offering state of the art equipment we would love to help you organize your event, and can offer you exclusive accommodation rates for your delegates. Full catering is available through our restaurant, so feel free to talk to us about your requirements.

We speak your language at BEST WESTERN Newmarket Inn & Suites, and look forward to welcoming you soon.

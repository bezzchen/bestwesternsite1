BEST WESTERN Newmarket Inn & Suites is located in the heart of Auckland's top Education, Medical and Business Centre. Just 5km from the CBD, and 800m from the popular Newmarket Shopping district making it the perfect accommodation option for your trip to Auckland. With a convenient location approximately 15km away from Auckland International and Domestic Airport, BEST WESTERN Newmarket Inn & Suites has to be in one of Auckland's top locations. Being on a major transportation route our proximity gives our guests easy access to major commercial, sporting, tourism, recreational and culture venues in Auckland.

Directions
If you are coming from State Highway 1 Take Market Rd exit off State Highway 1. Follow Epsom sign to traffic lights (100 metres). Turn right into Great South Rd. Our motel is just 250 metres on the right (112 Great South Road, Epsom). If you are coming from the Airport along Manukau Rd Drive to the very end of Manukau Road, and then turn right to Great South Rd. Our motel will be on your left. (112 Great South Road, Epsom). If you are driving from Auckland Airport, Make sure you search us on GPS with 'Epsom' as the area, and drive towards 'Auckland' direction
